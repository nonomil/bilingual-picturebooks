# Pip's Toy Box - Image Generation Plan

## Style Profile
- Art Style: Oxford/Cambridge textbook style, clean 2D digital illustration, soft pastel colors, minimal white background, high-quality, whimsical.
- Character: Pip (Small white bunny, cute, big expressive eyes, soft fur).
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9

## Page Sequence & Prompts
1. Page 1: "Hello! I am Pip." -> Pip waving a paw, looking at viewer, friendly.
2. Page 2: "I have a toy box." -> Pip standing next to a colorful wooden toy box.
3. Page 3: "I see one red car." -> Pip pointing at one bright red toy car.
4. Page 4: "I see two yellow ducks." -> Pip looking at two cute yellow rubber ducks.
5. Page 5: "I see three blue balls." -> Pip playing with three bright blue balls.
6. Page 6: "I see four green frogs." -> Pip surprised by four small green toy frogs.
7. Page 7: "I see five pink rabbits." -> Pip surrounded by five small pink toy rabbits.
8. Page 8: "I love my toys." -> Pip hugging the toy box, looking very happy.
9. Page 9: "Come and play with me!" -> Pip gesturing to the viewer, inviting them to play.
