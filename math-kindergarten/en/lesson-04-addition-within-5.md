# Lesson 4 — Addition Within 5

## 📋 Learning Goals
- Understand addition as "putting together"
- Recognize the plus sign `+` and equals sign `=`
- Add numbers within 5

## 💎 Number Bonds (Part-Part-Whole)

Numbers can be split into two smaller parts. These are called **Number Bonds**!

```
      5
     / \
    3   2
```

> 3 and 2 make 5. That's why 3 + 2 = 5!

Try these number bonds:

```
    4           3
   / \         / \
  1   ?       1   2
```

> Can you find the missing parts?

---

## ❌ Common Mistakes

- ❌ **Seeing "2 + 1" and only counting the 1**
✅ Addition means **putting together** — start with 2, then add 1 more → **3 total**.

- ❌ **Mixing up `+` and `=`**
✅ **`+`** means "plus" (add together). **`=`** means "equals" (result).
Example: **2 + 2 = 4** — "Two plus two equals four."



## 🧠 Think About It

1. **Observation**: If you have 3 pumpkins on the left and 2 on the right, together they make 5. Can you count them one by one to check?

2. **What if**: In "2 + 2 = 4", what if only 1 more apple came instead of 2? What would the equation be?



## 🔗 Cross-Curricular Links

- **Reading & Writing**: Practice sentences like: "2 apples plus 1 apple equals 3 apples."
  Vocabulary: **plus, equals, total, altogether**

- **Art**: Draw 3 red flowers and 2 yellow flowers. How many flowers total?
