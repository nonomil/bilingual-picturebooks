## Page 2: Happy and Sad 😊😢

> "Today, I feel **happy**!"

```
   H-A-P-P-Y → happy 😊
```

> "Happy is when you feel good. You smile and laugh."

```
   😊 Happy:
   - you smile 😊
   - you feel good
   - things are fun
   
   I am happy when I play with friends!
```

Steve jumps around. "Today is a happy day!"

But then Alex's face changes.

> "Why are you **sad**?" asks Steve.

```
   S-A-D → sad 😢
```

> "Sad is when you feel bad. You might cry."

```
   😢 Sad:
   - you feel down
   - you might cry
   - things feel hard
   
   It is okay to be sad sometimes.
```

Alex sighs. "I lost my favorite pencil..."

## Page 4: Angry and Scared 😠😱

After lunch, a bee buzzes near them.

> "Buzz buzz!"

Steve swats at the bee. "Go away!"

> "Why are you **angry**?" asks Alex.

```
   A-N-G-R-Y → angry 😠
```

> "Angry is when you feel mad. Your face gets hot."

```
   😠 Angry:
   - you feel mad
   - face gets hot
   - you might shout
   
   Take a deep breath when you are angry.
```

Steve takes a deep breath. In... out... "Okay, I'm not angry now."

```
   😱 Scared:

   - you want to hide
   
   It is okay to be scared.
```

> "We **must** go home before the storm!"

## Page 5: All Feelings Matter 💖

Back home, safe from the storm, they sit by the fire.

> "Today we felt so many feelings!" says Alex.

```
   😊 Happy — playing in the morning
   😢 Sad — losing my pencil
   😴 Tired — after searching
   🍽️ Hungry — before lunch
   😠 Angry — at the bee
   😱 Scared — of the thunder
```

> "Every feeling is okay," says Steve. "Feelings come and go, like clouds in the sky."

**Sight word: no**
> "There is **no** bad feeling. All feelings are real."

**no** = 不/没有 — not any

```
   No, thank you.
   I have no pencil.
   There is no rain now.
```

**Sight word: out**
> "When the sun comes **out**, let's play again!"

**out** = 外面 — not inside

```
   Go out and play!
   The sun came out.
   Look out the window.
```

The rain stops. The sun comes out. A rainbow appears.

> "Look! A rainbow!" 🌈

> "After rain, comes sun. After sad, comes happy."

## Page 6: The Feelings Game 🎲

The next day, the teacher plays a feelings game.

> "Pick a card. Act it out! We guess the feeling!"

```
   🎲 Feelings Game:
   
   😊 — "You are HAPPY!"
   😢 — "You are SAD!"
   😴 — "You are TIRED!"
   🍽️ — "You are HUNGRY!"
   😠 — "You are ANGRY!"
   😱 — "You are SCARED!"
```

Steve picks "tired". He yawns big and slumps down. "You are TIRED!" everyone shouts.

Alex picks "scared". She makes big eyes and hides behind her hands. "You are SCARED!"

Everyone takes turns. Laughter fills the classroom.

```
   💡 Feelings Lesson:
   
   All feelings are okay.
   Tell someone how you feel.
   Feelings change — like the weather!
```

### ⚠️ Common Mistakes

| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Right |
|----------|---------|
| "I feeled happy" | **"I felt happy"** — feel→felt (不规则过去式，不是加 -ed) |
| "I am feel sad" | **"I feel sad"** — feel 本身就是动词，不需要 am |
| "She angry" | **"She is angry"** — 形容词前面必须有 be 动词 (is/am/are) |
| "I am boring" (when you feel bored) | **"I am bored"** — boring = 令人无聊的，bored = 感到无聊的 |

### 🧠 Think About It
1. **SEL (Social-Emotional Learning)**: What can you do when you feel angry or scared? Can you think of three ways to feel calm?
2. **What if**: What if feelings were Minecraft potions? What color would a "calm potion" be? What about a "brave potion"?

## 🔗 Cross-Curricular Links

**美术**：画Feelings Rainbow——每种颜色代表一种情绪，英文写名字
