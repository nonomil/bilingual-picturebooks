## Page 1: A Special Day

Steve woke up feeling excited. "Today is a special day!"

He ran downstairs — but the house was quiet. No one was there.

> "**Where** is everyone?" Steve wondered. "Did they forget?"

He felt a little sad. But then he saw a note on the table:

> "Come to the park at noon. — Your friends"

Steve's eyes lit up. "They didn't forget!"

```
   H-A-P-P-Y → happy 😊
   
   Now Steve was HAPPY again!
```

**Sight word: who**
> "**Who** left this note?"

**who** = 谁 — which person

```
   Who is at the door? 🚪
   Who wants cake? 🎂
```

## Page 2: The Surprise

At noon, Steve walked **into** the park.

**Sight word: into**
> "I walked **into** the park and saw..."

**into** = 进入 — go inside

```
   Come into the room. 🚪
   Jump into the water. 🌊
```

> "SURPRISE!!!"

All his friends jumped out from behind the trees! Tom, Lily, Mia, Alex — everyone was there!

Balloons, streamers, and a big banner: "HAPPY BIRTHDAY STEVE!"

> "A **party**! For me!" Steve was shocked.

```
   P-A-R-T-Y → party 🎉
   
   party = 派对 — a fun gathering with friends
```

There was music, games, and a long table covered with food.

> "**Who** planned all this?" asked Steve.

> "We all did!" said Alex. "We **must** keep it a secret until today!"

**Sight word: must**
> "You **must** blow out the candles!"

**must** = 必须 — have to

```
   I must go now.
   You must try the cake!
```

## Page 3: Cake and Candles

Alex brought out a big **cake**.

```
   C-A-K-E → cake 🎂
```

It was a chocolate cake with white frosting. On top were six colorful **candles**.

```
   C-A-N-D-L-E → candle 🕯️
```

> "Make a wish!" everyone shouted.

Steve closed his eyes. "I wish... for more days like this. With my best friends."

He took a deep breath and blew out all the candles. WHOOSH!

Everyone clapped and cheered.

**Sight word: new**
> "I am six years old now! A **new** age!"

**new** = 新的 — not old, just got

```
   A new book! 📖
   Happy new year! 🎉
   New shoes! 👟
```

Then everyone sang.

```
   S-I-N-G → sing 🎵
   
   "Happy birthday to you!
   Happy birthday to you!
   Happy birthday, dear Steve!
   Happy birthday to you!"
```

## Page 4: Gifts and Thanks

After the cake, Steve opened his **gifts**.

```
   G-I-F-T → gift 🎁
```

From Alex: a new pencil set with every color! "For your drawings," she said.

From Tom: a soccer ball! "Now we can play every day!"

From Lily: a book about dragons! "Your favorite!"

From Mia: a handmade scarf! "For cold days," she said.

Steve's eyes got a little wet. "These are the best gifts ever. But the best gift is... having friends like you."

Everyone gathered for a group hug.

**Sight word: how**
> "**How** did you all keep this a secret?"

**how** = 怎么 — in what way

```
   How are you? 😊
   How did you do that? 🤔
   How big is the cake? 🎂
```

> "It wasn't easy!" laughed Alex. "But seeing your face made it all worth it!"

```
   🎁 Gifts from friends:
   
   ✏️ pencils — from Alex
   ⚽ ball — from Tom
   📖 book — from Lily
   🧣 scarf — from Mia
   ❤️ friendship — from everyone!
```

## Page 5: The Best Birthday

As the party wound down, Steve sat with Alex on the park bench.

> "This was the best birthday ever," said Steve. "I was sad this morning... I thought everyone forgot."

> "We would never forget you," said Alex. "You are **our** best friend."

> "And **how** do you feel now?" she asked.

> "**Happy**," said Steve. "The **happiest** I have ever been."

The sun was setting. The balloons swayed in the breeze. The empty cake plate sat on the table. And Steve had a smile that wouldn't go away.

### ⚠️ Common Mistakes

| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Right |
|----------|---------|
| "She gift me a pencil" | **"She gave me a pencil"** — gift is a noun, give/gave is the verb |
| "Six candle on the cake" | **"Six candles on the cake"** — add -s for plural after numbers > 1 |
| "I very happy at the party" | **"I am very happy at the party"** — English needs the verb "to be" (am/is/are) |
| "The children singed the song" | **"The children sang the song"** — sing→sang is irregular |

### 🧠 Think About It
1. **Surprise**: Steve felt sad in the morning because he thought everyone forgot his birthday. Why do surprise parties make you feel sad before happy?
2. **Gratitude**: Steve said the best gift was having friends like Alex, Tom, Lily, and Mia. What is the best gift you can give someone without spending money?
