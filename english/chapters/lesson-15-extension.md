# Lesson 15 Extension: The Feelings Rainbow 🌈

## 📋 Learning Goals
- Review feelings: happy, sad, tired, hungry, angry, scared
- Review sight words: been, must, no, now, on, out
- New words: **brave, kind, calm, love, hug**
- Sentence: "I feel ___ when ___."
- 🔤 Sound Block review: -y /i/ + -ly /li/ introduction

## 🔤 Sound Block Review: y at the end

```
   🎵 NOTE BLOCK GLOWING!

   y at end of word = /i/:
   happy 😊  baby 👶  yummy 😋  sunny ☀️  funny 😂

   -ly at end of word = /li/ (new!):
   slow-ly → slowly! 🐢
   kind-ly → kindly! 💝
   soft-ly → softly! 🪶
   loud-ly → loudly! 📢
   quiet-ly → quietly! 🤫
```

## Page 1: The Feelings Wall 🎨

After the feelings game, the teacher has an idea.

> "Let's make a Feelings Wall! Every day, we put our feeling on the wall."

She hands out paper and crayons. "Draw your feeling. Use colors!"

```
   🎨 Feelings Wall:
   
   😊 Happy — draw something yellow ☀️
   😢 Sad — draw something blue 💧
   😠 Angry — draw something red 🔴
   😱 Scared — draw something purple 🟣
   😴 Tired — draw something grey 🩶
   🍽️ Hungry — draw something orange 🟠
```

Steve draws a big yellow sun. "Happy!"

Alex draws a blue cloud with rain. "I felt sad this morning... but now I'm better!"

> "That's okay!" says the teacher. "You can feel two things in one day!"

## Page 2: Brave 😤

> "What about when you feel scared, but you do it anyway?"

The teacher writes a new word on the board:

```
   B-R-A-V-E → brave 😤
```

> "Brave means you feel scared, but you still try!"

```
   😤 Brave:
   - feel scared inside
   - but you do it anyway
   - you are strong
   
   "I was scared to swim, but I was brave!"
```

Steve remembers his first swim lesson. "I was scared of the water. But I was brave and jumped in!"

Alex remembers her first day at school. "I was scared to meet new friends. But I was brave and said hello!"

```
   💪 Bravery isn't having no fear —
   it's doing it even when you ARE scared.
```

## Page 3: Kind and Calm 💝😌

> "And what do you do when your friend is sad?"

```
   K-I-N-D → kind 💝
```

> "Kind means you are nice to others. You help them feel better."

```
   💝 Kind:
   - you help others
   - you say nice things
   - you share
   
   "Be kind to your friends!"
```

> "When Alex was sad, Steve was kind," says the teacher. "He helped find the pencil."

Alex nods. "That made me feel **calm**."

```
   C-A-L-M → calm 😌
```

> "Calm means you feel peaceful. Not angry, not scared — just quiet and okay."

```
   😌 Calm:
   - peaceful
   - quiet inside
   - no big feelings
   
   After a storm comes calm.
   Deep breath makes you calm.
```

They practice: close eyes, breathe in... out... "Calm."

> 📐 **CEFR Level:** Pre-A1 / A1 | **对标:** 英语课标一级·听说·动作/地点/感受/时间表达

### ⚠️ Common Mistakes

| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Right |
|----------|---------|
| "I feeled happy" | **"I felt happy"** — feel → felt (不规则过去式) |
| "I am feel sad" | **"I feel sad"** — feel 本身就是动词，不需要 am |
| "She angry" | **"She is angry"** — 形容词前面必须有 be 动词 |
| "I am boring" (when you feel bored) | **"I am bored"** — boring = 令人无聊的，bored = 感到无聊的 |
### 🧠 Think About It
1. **Pattern**: "I walk → I walked" but "I go → I went, I see → I saw". Why are some past tense words so different?
2. **What if**: If Steve could only use 10 English words to survive in the Minecraft village, which 10 would he pick?
