# Lesson 10 Extension: The Toy Maker's Workshop 🔨

## 📋 Learning Goals
- Review toy & school words: ball, doll, car, book, pencil, bag
- Review sight words: away, jump, me, new, this
- New words: **toy, make, build, puzzle, kite**
- Sentence: "I can make a ___." / "Let's build ___."
- 🔤 Sound Block review: th /θ/ + th says /ð/ (the, this, that, they)

---

## 🔤 Sound Block Review: Two "th" Sounds!

```
   🎵 NOTE BLOCK GLOWING!

   th has TWO sounds!
   
   th = /θ/ (tongue out, blow air — "thin" th)
   three 3️⃣  thank 🙏  think 🤔  thin 📏  bath 🛁  path 🛤️
   
   th = /ð/ (tongue out, voice on — "the" th)
   the     this 👆  that 👉  they 👥  there 📍  mother 👩
   
   Touch your throat:
   /θ/ → no buzz  (thin)
   /ð/ → buzz!    (this)
```

## Page 1: The Toy Maker's Shop 🏪

Walking through the village, Steve and Alex find a new shop.

> "The Toy Maker's Workshop!" reads the sign.

```
   🏪 THE TOY MAKER'S WORKSHOP
   
   We MAKE toys! 🧸
   Come and BUILD with us! 🔨
```

Inside, an old villager with glasses is carving wood. Wood shavings are everywhere. Shelves are full of colorful toys.

> "Welcome! I am the **toy** maker!"

```
   T-O-Y → toy 🧸
   
   toy = 玩具 — something you play with
   
   TOYS are for fun!
```

> "Do you want to see how I **make** toys?"

```
   M-A-K-E → make 🔨
   
   make = 制作 — create, build
```

Steve and Alex nod excitedly.

## Page 2: How to Make a Toy Car 🚗

> "First, let me show you how to **build** a toy car!"

```
   B-U-I-L-D → build 🏗️
   
   build = 建造 — put parts together
```

The toy maker picks up a block of wood.

```
   🚗 Building a Toy Car:
   
   1. Cut the wood 🪚
   2. Sand it smooth 🧽
   3. Add four wheels 🛞🛞🛞🛞
   4. Paint it red 🎨
   5. Let it dry ☀️
   
   = A NEW toy car! 🚗
```

> "Every toy I make is different. Some are fast, some are slow. Some are big, some are little."

He gives Steve a block of wood and a small saw.

> "Your turn! Try to make your own!"

Steve carefully cuts and sands the wood. Alex helps attach the wheels.

> "Look! We built a car!" Steve holds it up proudly.

```
   🚗 Our Toy Car:
   - wooden body
   - four wheels
   - painted BLUE
   - goes ZOOM! 💨
```

## Page 3: The Puzzle Corner 🧩

In the corner of the shop, Alex finds a table covered with little pieces.

> "What is this?" she asks.

> "That is a **puzzle**!" says the toy maker.

```
   P-U-Z-Z-L-E → puzzle 🧩
```

> "A puzzle has many pieces. You put them together to make a picture."

```
   🧩 Puzzle:
   - many little pieces
   - put them together
   - make a picture!
```

Alex starts putting pieces together. A piece with blue sky... a piece with green grass... a piece with a yellow sun...

> "This piece goes here... and this one goes there..."

Bit by bit, the picture appears: a Minecraft village with all the animals!

> "I did it!" Alex cheers. "I finished the puzzle!"

The toy maker smiles. "Puzzles teach you to be patient. Good job!"

```
   🧩 Proverb:
   Life is like a puzzle.
   Every piece has its place.
```

## Page 4: Kite Making 🪁

> "What about something that flies?" asks Steve.

The toy maker's eyes light up. "Ah! Let's make a **kite**!"

```
   K-I-T-E → kite 🪁
```

He takes out paper, thin sticks, string, and colorful cloth.

```
   🪁 Making a Kite:
   
   1. Make a cross with two sticks ✝️
   2. Tie them together 🪢
   3. Cut the paper to shape ✂️
   4. Glue it on 🩹
   5. Add a long tail 📏
   6. Tie the string 🧵
   
   = A FLYING kite! 🪁☁️
```

Steve and Alex each make a kite. Steve's is blue like the sky. Alex's is red like a flower.

> "Let's fly them!"

Outside the shop, the wind blows gently. They run with their kites.

Steve's kite goes up... up... UP! 🪁
Alex's kite dances in the wind! 🪁

> "Look how high they fly!"

```
   🪁 Kite flying needs:
   ☀️ a clear day
   💨 some wind
   🏃 running fast
   😊 a big smile!
```

## Page 5: The Best Toy of All 🎁

At the end of the day, Steve and Alex have made three things:

```
   🚗 a wooden car
   🧩 a finished puzzle
   🪁 a flying kite
```

> "Which toy do you like best?" asks the toy maker.

Steve thinks. "I like the car I made — because I built it with my own hands!"

Alex thinks. "I like the kite — because it flies so high!"

The toy maker nods. "You know what the best toy is?"

> "The one you make yourself! Because it has a piece of your heart in it."

He gives them each a small wooden block. "This is for your next toy. Use your imagination!"

```
   💡 The best toys:
   
   Not the most expensive 🚫💰
   Not the biggest 🚫🐘
   Not the shiniest 🚫✨
   
   But the ones YOU make
   With YOUR hands
   And YOUR heart ❤️
```

Steve looks at his wooden block. "What should I make?"

> "Anything you can imagine," says the toy maker.

## Page 6: Story Time — The Night the Toys Came Alive 🌙

That night, Steve puts his new wooden car on his bedside table.

He falls asleep... and dreams.

In his dream, all the toys come alive!

```
   🌙 The Toys Awake!
   
   ⚽ Ball: bouncing around the room
   🪆 Doll: dancing on the shelf
   🚗 Wooden car: ZOOMING across the floor
   🧩 Puzzle: putting itself together
   🪁 Kite: floating near the ceiling
```

> "Steve! Steve! Come play with us!" the toys call.

Dream Steve jumps out of bed. "Let's play!"

The ball and car have a race. The doll cheers. The puzzle flies apart and puts itself back together like magic. The kite carries Dream Steve up to the ceiling for a bird's-eye view!

> "This is the best dream ever!"

Just then, the wooden car bumps into the wall and... TOOT! Its horn honks.

Steve wakes up, blinking. It is morning. The toys are still on the table — quiet, not moving.

> "Was it a dream?" Steve wonders.

But then he notices — the wooden car is not where he left it. It is on the floor, next to the ball.

> "Hmm..." Steve smiles. "Maybe it wasn't just a dream."

```
   🧸 Some say...
   Toys play at night
   When the moon is bright
   And children sleep tight 🌙
```
