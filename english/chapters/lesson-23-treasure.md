## 📖 The Treasure — A Story

---

## Page 1: The Old Map

Steve and Alex found something in Grandpa's barn — an old box covered in dust.

Inside the box: a rolled-up piece of paper.

> "It's a **map**!" said Alex, unrolling it carefully.

```
   M-A-P → map 🗺️
```

The map was old and yellow. It showed the village, the farm, the mountain, and a big red X.

> "X marks the spot!" said Steve. "There is treasure hidden somewhere!"

> "**Read** what the map says," said Alex.

At the bottom of the map, in tiny letters:

> "Follow the path through the enchanted garden. Open the stone door with the golden **key**. Inside you shall find the treasure of old."

**Sight word: read**
> "I can **read** it!" said Steve.

**read** = 读 — look at words and understand

```
   Read a book. 📖
   Can you read this? ❓
   I love to read. 📚
```

## Page 2: Into the Garden

They followed the map into the woods behind Grandpa's farm. A stone gate covered in vines marked the entrance to the old garden.

> "This must be it!" said Alex.

They **walked** through the garden. Old statues, stone benches, a dried-up fountain.

The path ended at a stone wall. On the wall: a carving of a keyhole.

> "We need a **key**!"

```
   K-E-Y → key 🔑
```

> "The map said 'golden key'. Where is it?"

Alex looked around. Near the fountain, something glinted in the sun. She dug it out of the dirt —

A small, golden key!

> "I **found** it!"

```
   F-O-U-N-D → found ✅
   
   found = find 的过去式
```

**Sight word: upon**
> "**Upon** the key, there were tiny words: 'For those who seek.'"

**upon** = 在…上 — on, on top of (old-fashioned "on")

```
   Once upon a time... 📖
   The cat sat upon the wall. 🐱
   Upon my word! ✋
```

## Page 3: The Treasure Chamber

Click. The key turned in the stone keyhole. The wall rumbled and slid open.

Inside: a small chamber lit by glowing crystals.

In the center of the room sat a wooden **chest**.

```
   C-H-E-S-T → chest 📦
   
   chest = 箱子 — a box for keeping things
```

> "The treasure **chest**!" Steve whispered.

With trembling hands, they lifted the lid.

Inside — not **gold** coins, not jewels —

```
   G-O-L-D → gold 🥇
```

But books! Beautiful leather-bound books with golden letters.

> "A library of old stories!" Alex gasped.

**Sight word: tell**
> "These books **tell** the history of the village!"

**tell** = 告诉 — say to someone, narrate

```
   Tell me a story. 📖
   Can you tell me the way? 🗺️
   I will tell you a secret. 🤫
```

One book was titled "The First Days of Our Village". Another: "The Great Storm of Long Ago". A third: "Famous Adventures of the Mountain People".

> "This isn't gold or jewels," said Alex. "It's something better — knowledge."

<img src="./img/chapter-23-en/page-03.png" width="480" alt="Minecraft glowing crystal chamber, wooden chest, books with golden letters, Steve and Alex reading old village history books"/>

## Page 4: The Real Treasure

They sat in the chamber and read story after story. Stories of brave villagers. Stories of magical creatures. Stories of friendship and adventure.

> "**Upon** the map, it said 'treasure of old'," said Steve. "Stories are the oldest treasure there is."

They **took** a few books back to the village. The librarian's eyes went wide.

> "These are the lost histories! We thought they were destroyed in the great fire!"

Everyone in the village gathered to hear the stories **read** aloud. Old people remembered. Young people learned.

**Sight word: wish**
> "I **wish** everyone could read these stories," said Alex.

**wish** = 希望 — want something, hope

```
   I wish upon a star. ⭐
   Make a wish! 🎂
   I wish you were here. 💌
```

Steve smiled. "Now they can. Because we found them."

```
   🔮 Real Treasure:
   
   Not gold 💰
   Not jewels 💎
   But STORIES — 
   that live forever. 📚❤️
```
