## 📖 At the Farm — A Story

---

## Page 1: The Invitation

Steve got a letter. It was from his grandpa.

> "Dear Steve, come visit the **farm**! The animals miss you. — Grandpa"

Steve was so happy. He **asked** Alex to come too.

```
   A-S-K → ask ❓
   
   ask = 问 — say a question, request
```

> "Can I bring my friend Alex?" Steve **asked** his grandpa on the phone.

> "Of course!" said Grandpa. "The more, the better!"

**Sight word: let**
> "Grandpa **let** us visit the farm!"

**let** = 让 — allow, permit

```
   Let me help you.
   Let's go!
   Let him come in.
```

They packed their bags and took the bus to the farm.

## Page 2: The Farm Animals

Grandpa met them at the gate. "Welcome! Let me show you around."

First, they saw a big brown **cow**.

> "Mooo!" said the cow.

```
   C-O-W → cow 🐄
```

> "This is Bessie. She gives us milk every morning."

Next, they saw a pink **pig** rolling in the mud.

> "Oink oink!"

```
   P-I-G → pig 🐷
```

> "Pigs love mud," said Grandpa. "It keeps them cool."

Then, a fluffy **sheep** with thick wool.

> "Baaa!"

```
   S-H-E-E-P → sheep 🐑
```

> "We cut their wool to make warm clothes."

**Sight word: may**
> "**May** I pet the sheep?" asked Alex.

**may** = 可以 — allowed to, can

```
   May I come in? 🚪
   May I have some water? 💧
   You may sit here. 🪑
```

## Page 3: Hen and Horse

In the chicken coop, they found a busy red **hen**.

> "Cluck cluck cluck!"

```
   H-E-N → hen 🐔
```

> "Hens give us eggs," said Grandpa. He reached into a nest and pulled out a warm brown egg. "Fresh this morning!"

Then Grandpa whistled. A beautiful brown **horse** trotted over.

```
   H-O-R-S-E → horse 🐴
```

> "This is Lightning. He's an **old** horse, but he's still strong."

**Sight word: old**
> "Lightning is an **old** horse, but a good friend."

**old** = 老 — not young, has been around long

```
   An old book. 📖
   My old friend. 👫
   How old are you? 🎂
```

> "**May** I ride Lightning?" Steve asked.

> "Of course you **may**," said Grandpa.

Steve climbed on Lightning's back. The horse walked slowly around the field. Steve felt like a real farmer!

## Page 4: The Farmer's Life

After the tour, they sat on the porch with cold lemonade.

```
   F-A-R-M-E-R → farmer 👨‍🌾
```

> "Grandpa, you have been a **farmer** for a long time," said Steve.

> "Yes, my **own** farm for forty years," said Grandpa.

**Sight word: own**
> "This is my **own** farm. I built it myself."

**own** = 自己的 — belonging to oneself

```
   My own room. 🛏️
   Your own book. 📖
   He has his own bike. 🚲
```

> "It's hard work," Grandpa said. "But when you love what you do, it doesn't feel like work."

Alex looked at Steve. "Your grandpa is the wisest person I know."

> "I just **ask** the animals," Grandpa laughed. "They always have the answers."

The sun set over the fields. The cow mooed. The sheep baaed. The horse neighed. It was a farm symphony.

### ⚠️ Common Mistakes

| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Right |
|----------|---------|
| "He don't like it" | **"He doesn't like it"** — he/she/it uses doesn't |
| "There is many blocks" | **"There are many blocks"** — many = plural = are |
| "Can you help me?" "Yes, I help" | **"Yes, I can"** or **"Yes, I will help you"** |
| Reading word by word, not in phrases | Read in **meaning chunks**: "Steve walked / into the cave / and saw a light" |
