## Page 1: The Hungry Villagers 🍽️

Steve and Alex are walking through the village. Steve's stomach growls.

> "Grrrrrowl..."

> "Are you hungry?" asks Alex.

> "Yes! I want food!"

Just then, a villager opens a door. "Come in! We have a big lunch today!"

Inside, a long table is covered with food. So many colors and smells!

```
   🍽️ FOOD TABLE 🍽️
   
   Today's food:

   🍞 bread     🥚 egg       🎂 cake
   💧 water
```

**eat** = 吃 — put food in your mouth

```

   I eat bread. 🍞
   Let's eat! 🍽️
```

## Page 2: Apple and Banana 🍎🍌

Steve picks up a red fruit.

> "An **apple**! I love apples!"

```
   A-P-P-L-E → apple 🍎
```

> "The apple is red and round. It is sweet and crunchy!"

```
   🍎 Apple:
   - red, green, or yellow
   - round
   - sweet and crunchy
   
   One apple a day keeps the doctor away!
```

Alex grabs a long yellow fruit.

> "A **banana**! My favorite!"

```
   B-A-N-A-N-A → banana 🍌
```

She peels it. "Bananas are yellow and soft."

> "Monkeys love bananas. I love bananas too!"

**Sight word: like**
> "I **like** apples and bananas!"

**like** = 喜欢 — enjoy, think it is good

```
   I like apples. 🍎
   I like bananas. 🍌
   Do you like apples?
```

## Page 3: Milk and Bread 🥛🍞

A villager pours white liquid into two cups.

> "Here is fresh **milk**!"

```
   M-I-L-K → milk 🥛
```

```
   I get bread. 🍞
```

## Page 8: Story Time — The Very Hungry Enderman 🖤

After lunch, Steve and Alex sit under a tree. Suddenly, a tall dark figure appears.

> "Ender... man..." it whispers.

> "An Enderman!" Alex gasps. "But he looks sad."

The Enderman's stomach growls. It is hungry!

> "Do you want food?" asks Steve.

The Enderman nods sadly.

Steve offers an apple. The Enderman looks at it. "Ender... apple?"

He takes a bite. His eyes turn from purple to HAPPY purple! 💜✨

Then a banana. "Ender... banana!" 🍌✨

Then bread. Milk. Cake!

> "Ender... LIKE... food!"

The Enderman is so happy, he picks up a flower block and gives it to Steve. "Ender... thank... you."

> "Even Endermen get hungry!" Alex laughs.

The Enderman waves goodbye and teleports away — but first, he grabs one more apple. 🍎

> "I think we made a friend," says Steve.

```
   🖤 Enderman's Food List:
   🍎 apple  — YES!
   🍌 banana — YES!
   🍞 bread  — YES!
   🎂 cake   — YES YES YES!
```

### ⚠️ Common Mistakes

| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Right |
|----------|---------|
| "I like apple" (talking about apples in general) | **"I like apples"** — 泛指一类东西用复数 |
| "Give me a milk" | **"Give me some milk"** — milk 不可数，用 "some" |
| "I want eat cake" | **"I want TO eat cake"** — want + TO + verb |
| "The apple red" | **"The apple is red"** — 别忘了 be 动词 (is/am/are) |

### 🧠 Think About It
1. **Nutrition**: Why do we need to eat different kinds of food? What does each food give our body?
2. **What if**: What if the Hungry Enderman only ate ONE type of food? What would happen to him?
