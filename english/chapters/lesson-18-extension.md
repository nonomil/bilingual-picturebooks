## 🎣 Page 2: Game Time!

**① Word Fishing**: Fish a word card from the pond. Say it and do the action!

> Steve fishes: "RUN!" — He runs in place. ✅
> Alex fishes: "HAPPY!" — She makes a big smile. ✅

**② Feeling Ring Toss**: Toss a ring onto a feeling word. Make that face!

> Tom tosses: "SCARED!" — He makes a scared face. 😱
> Lily tosses: "BRAVE!" — She stands tall. 😤

**③ Place Hopscotch**: Hop on place words and say what you do there!

> "HOME!" 🏠 — "I sleep at home!"
> "SCHOOL!" 🏫 — "I learn at school!"
> "FARM!" 🐄 — "I see animals!"

**④ Transport Race**: Pick a vehicle and race while saying its name!

> Steve: "I RIDE my BIKE!" 🚲 (pedaling)
> Alex: "I FLY a PLANE!" ✈️ (arms out)

**⑤ Time Wheel**: Spin and say what you do at that time!

> Spin: "MORNING!" 🌅 — "I wake up and eat breakfast!"

**⑥ Big Sentence Train**: Each person adds one word to make a long sentence!

> "On" — "Saturday" — "I" — "went" — "to" — "the" — "park" — "with" — "my" — "bike"!

### ⚠️ Common Mistakes — Top 5 Carnival Review

| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Right |
|----------|---------|
| "I have 3 book" | **"I have 3 books"** — 复数加 -s（L3-L6 最高频错误） |
| "He go to the park" | **"He goes to the park"** — 第三人称单数 + s（L7-L12 最高频错误） |
| "I goed to school yesterday" | **"I went to school yesterday"** — go → went 不规则（L13-L17 新学） |
| "There is many animals" | **"There are many animals"** — many = 复数 = are |
| "I can to swim" | **"I can swim"** — 情态动词后不加 to |
### 🧠 Think About It
1. **Pattern**: "I walk → I walked" but "I go → I went, I see → I saw". Why are some past tense words so different?
2. **What if**: If Steve could only use 10 English words to survive in the Minecraft village, which 10 would he pick?
