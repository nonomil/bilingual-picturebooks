## Page 2: Cat 🐱

The first animal they see is sitting on a wooden fence.

> "Meow!" 

> "A **cat**!" says Steve. "A little orange cat!"

```
   C-A-T → cat 🐱
```

The cat jumps down and rubs against Steve's legs.

> "The cat is so friendly!"

**Sight word: play**
> "The cat wants to **play**!"

**play** = 玩 — have fun, do something fun

```
   The cat can play.
   Let's play!
   I play with my sister.
```

The ranger gives them a toy mouse. The cat chases it around. 🐱🏃‍♂️🧸

## Page 3: Dog 🐶

Next, a happy animal runs up to them. Wagging its tail!

> "The dog can **run** very fast!"

```
   The dog can run. 🐶💨

   Run with me!
```

Steve throws a stick. The dog runs after it — zoom! — and brings it back.

> "The dog can run AND play!"

## Page 4: Bird 🐦

High in a tree, they hear singing.

> "Tweet! Tweet! Chirp!"

> "A **bird**!" says Steve, looking up.

```
   B-I-R-D → bird 🐦
```

A blue bird sits on a branch, singing a happy song. Next to it is a nest with three little eggs.

> "The bird made a nest," says the ranger. "Soon, baby birds will come!"

**Sight word: said**
> "'Hello, little bird!' **said** Alex."

**said** = 说 — past tense of "say"

```
   Mom said, "Come here."
   I said, "Hello!"
   The bird said, "Tweet tweet!"
```

**Sight word: make**
> "The bird can **make** a nest."

**make** = 做、制作 — create, build

```
   Birds make nests.
   I can make a cake.
   Let's make a picture!
```

## Page 8: The Animal Song 🎵

As the sun sets, Steve and Alex sit by the pond. All the animals have come out to say goodbye.

```
   🐱 Cat on the fence — meow meow
   🐶 Dog by the tree — woof woof
   🐦 Bird in the branch — tweet tweet
   🐟 Fish in the pond — splash splash
   🐰 Rabbit in the grass — hop hop
   🦆 Duck on the water — quack quack
```

They make up a song:

```
   🎵 Little cat, little cat, what do you say?
      "Meow meow meow!" — I can play!
   
      Happy dog, happy dog, what do you do?
      "Woof woof woof!" — I run with you!
   
      Blue bird, blue bird, in the tree,
      "Tweet tweet tweet!" — sing for me!
   
      Orange fish, orange fish, in the pond,
      Swim swim swim — and go beyond!
      
      Fluffy rabbit, fluffy rabbit, hop hop hop,
      Little duck, little duck, never stop! 🎵
```

Steve writes in his notebook:

> "Today I saw six animals in the forest. The cat can play. The dog can run. The bird can make a nest. The fish can swim. The rabbit can hop. The duck can do everything!"

> "Animals are our friends," says Alex.

> "Yes," the ranger smiles. "Take care of them. They are little, but they have big hearts."

### ⚠️ Common Mistakes

| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Right |
|----------|---------|
| "I see 3 cat" | **"I see 3 cats"** — numbers + plural -s (一只猫→两只猫，英文要加s) |
| "The dog can run fast. He run fast." | **"The dog runs fast"** — he/she/it + verb+s |
| "A bird can to fly" | **"A bird can fly"** — can 后面不加 to，超级英雄规则 |
| "I has a rabbit" | **"I have a rabbit"** — I/you/we/they → have; he/she/it → has |

### 🧠 Think About It
1. **Science**: Why do different animals make different sounds? (Cat meows, dog barks, duck quacks — each animal's mouth is shaped differently!)
2. **What if**: What if animals could talk in English? What would the cat say to the dog in the Animal Forest?
