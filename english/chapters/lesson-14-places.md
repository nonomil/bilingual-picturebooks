## Page 1: Where Do You Go? 🗺️

Steve and Alex sit on a bench in the village square. A big map is on the wall.

> "Look at this map!" says Alex. "It shows all the **places** in town!"

```
   place = 地方
```

Steve points at the map. "So many places! Where should we go today?"

```
   🗺️ Places Around Town:
   
   🏠 home     🏫 school    🌳 park
   🏪 shop     🐄 farm      🦁 zoo
```

**Sight word: there**
> "I go **there** every day!" (pointing to school)

**there** = 那里 — at that place

```

   Look over there! 👀
   Put the book there. 📖
```

## Page 2: Home and School 🏠🏫

> "First, let's visit my **home**!" says Steve.

```
   H-O-M-E → home 🏠
```

> "Home is where you live. It is where your family is."

```
   🏠 Home:
   - where you live
   - where your family is
   - your safe place
   
   I go home after school.
```

Steve shows Alex his small wooden house. It has a red door and a garden with flowers.

Then they walk down the road.

> "And this is our **school**!"

```
   S-C-H-O-O-L → school 🏫
```

> "School is where you learn. You have a teacher and friends there."

```
   🏫 School:
   - where you learn
   - you have a teacher
   - you meet friends
   
   I go to school every day.
```

**Sight word: but**
> "I like home, **but** I like school too!"

**but** = 但是 — shows a difference

```
   I am tired, but happy.
   It is cold, but sunny.
   The book is old, but good.
```

## Page 7: Story Time — The Lost Map 🗺️

One weekend, Steve and Alex found an old map in the library.

> "It shows a secret path through town!" says Alex.

Step 1: Start at Home 🏠
Step 2: Walk past the School 🏫
Step 3: Turn left at the Park 🌳
Step 4: Stop at the Shop for snacks 🏪
Step 5: Follow the path to the Farm 🐄
Step 6: End at the Zoo! 🦁

They followed every step. Past the school... turn at the park... stop at the shop...

At the farm, they found a tiny wooden box under the big oak tree.

Inside the box: a key! But a key to what?

They ran to the zoo. At the very back, behind the monkey house, was a little door with a lock.

The key fit perfectly! Click!

Inside was a secret garden — full of the **prettiest** flowers they had ever seen.

> "The map **was** right!" said Steve. "There **was** a treasure!"

> "The **best** treasure isn't gold," said Alex. "It's finding a new place!"

They sat in the secret garden until the stars came out. **Dark** sky, bright stars, two friends, one perfect adventure.

### ⚠️ Common Mistakes

| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Right |
|----------|---------|
| "I go to school every days" | **"I go to school every day"** — every + 单数 |
| "I go to the school" (when school is an activity) | **"I go to school"** — go to school/hospital/work 不加 the |
| "There is many places in town" | **"There are many places"** — many + 复数 = are |
| "I live in the Beijing" | **"I live in Beijing"** — 城市名前不加 the |

### 🧠 Think About It
1. **Social Studies**: Why do towns have different places (school, park, shop, zoo)? What would happen if a town had no park?
2. **What if**: What if the Lost Map led to a place you designed yourself? What would be at the end of the path?
