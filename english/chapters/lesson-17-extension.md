## Page 1: Where Do They Go? 🛤️

After the transport race, Steve and Alex make a big chart.

> "Every vehicle has its own way to go!"

```
   🛤️ Where Vehicles Go:
   
   🚗 car → on the ROAD
   🚌 bus → on the ROAD
   🚂 train → on the TRACK
   🚤 boat → on the WATER
   ✈️ plane → in the SKY
   🚲 bike → on the ROAD

```
   R-O-A-D → road 🛤️
   T-R-A-C-K → track 🛤️

   W-A-T-E-R → water 🌊

## Page 2: Wheels and Wings 🛞🪶

> "What makes them go?"

```
   W-H-E-E-L → wheel 🛞
```

> "Cars, buses, trains, and bikes all have wheels. Wheels go round and round!"

```
   🛞 Vehicles with WHEELS:
   🚗 car — 4 wheels
   🚌 bus — 4 wheels (big!)
   🚂 train — many wheels on tracks
   🚲 bike — 2 wheels
```

> "But planes don't have wheels for going — they have **wings**!"

```
   F-L-Y → fly ✈️
   
   Planes fly in the sky.
   Birds fly too! 🐦
```

```
   🛞 Wheels: roll on ground
   🪶 Wings: fly in sky
   🚤 Boats: float on water (no wheels or wings!)
```

## Page 3: Traffic Light Game 🚦

The teacher sets up a game in the playground.

> "Red light — stop! Green light — go! Yellow light — slow down!"

```
   🚦 Traffic Light Game:
   
   🔴 RED — STOP! (freeze)
   🟡 YELLOW — SLOW! (walk slowly)
   🟢 GREEN — GO! (move fast)
```

Kids pretend to drive cars, ride bikes, and fly planes.

> "Red light!" — Everyone freezes mid-action!
> "Green light!" — Vroom! Ding-ding! Zoom!

```
   🚦 Traffic Light Words:
   STOP ✋  GO 🏃  SLOW 🐢  FAST ⚡
```

**Sight word review:**
```
   HE stops at the red light. 🔴
   SHE goes on the green light. 🟢
   THEY wait at the yellow light. 🟡
   THIS is a fun game!
   THOSE kids are playing too!
```
