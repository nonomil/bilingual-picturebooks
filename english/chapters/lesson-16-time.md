## Page 1: What Time Is It? ⏰

Steve looks at his clock. "What time is it?"

Alex points at the sun outside. "Use the sun! When it is low, it is **morning**. When it is high, it is **afternoon**. When it goes down, it is **night**."

```
   ⏰ Times of Day:
   
   🌅 morning — sun comes up
   ☀️ afternoon — sun is high
   🌙 night — sun goes down, moon comes out
```

**Sight word: be**
> "What will the weather **be** today?"

**be** = 是/在 — is, am, are (all forms)

```
   I will be there.
   Be happy!
   It can be fun.
```

## Page 2: Morning 🌅

> "In the **morning**, the sun comes up. A new **day** begins!"

```
   M-O-R-N-I-N-G → morning 🌅
   D-A-Y → day 🌅
```

> "Morning is the first part of the day. You wake up, eat breakfast, and get ready."

```
   🌅 Morning:
   - sun comes up
   - wake up 🛌→🚶
   - eat breakfast 🥣
   - go to school 🏫
   
   Good morning!
```

Steve stretches. "I love morning! The air is fresh and the birds sing."

```
   🌅 My Morning:
   I wake up.
   I eat breakfast.
   I get dressed.
   I go to school.
```

**Sight word: get**
> "I **get** up at seven in the morning."

**get** = 得到/变得 — receive, become

```
   Get up! (wake up)
   Get ready! (prepare)
   Get the book. (take)
```

## Page 5: Today and Tomorrow 📅

The next morning, Steve and Alex make plans.

> "**Today** we go to the zoo!"

```
   T-O-D-A-Y → today 📅
```

> "Today is the day right now. Not yesterday, not tomorrow — THIS day."

```
   📅 Today:
   - this day
   - happening now
   - it is today!
   
   What day is today?
   Today is Friday!
```

> "And **tomorrow** we go to the farm!"

```
   T-O-M-O-R-R-O-W → tomorrow 📆
```

> "Tomorrow is the day after today. It is in the future."

```
   📆 Tomorrow:
   - the day after today
   - in the future
   - not here yet
   
   See you tomorrow!
```

**Sight word: soon**
> "Tomorrow will come **soon**! Let's sleep now."

```
   soon = 很快 — in a short time
```

## Page 6: The Day Wheel 🎡

The teacher brings a big wheel to class.

> "This is the Day Wheel! Let's spin it to talk about time!"

```
   🎡 The Day Wheel:
   
   🌅 MORNING → ☀️ AFTERNOON → 🌙 NIGHT
        ↑                          ↓
        ← ← ←  A NEW DAY!  ← ← ← ←
```

The teacher spins the wheel. "What do you do in the morning?"

> "I wake up and eat breakfast!"

Spin again. "What do you do in the afternoon?"

> "I play and do homework!"

Spin again. "What do you do at night?"

> "I eat dinner and sleep!"

```
   ⏰ My Day:
   
   🌅 Morning:   wake up, eat, go to school
   ☀️ Afternoon: learn, play, go home
   🌙 Night:     eat dinner, read, sleep
   
   → And tomorrow, do it all again!
```

## Page 7: Story Time — The Mixed-Up Clock ⏲️

One day, the village clock tower broke! The hands started spinning backwards.

> "Oh no!" said the clock keeper. "Time is all mixed up!"

The sun rose at night. The moon came out in the morning. Birds sang at midnight!

> "We must fix the clock!" said Steve.

Alex had an idea. "Let's use the Day Wheel! If we can remember what happens when, we can set the clock right."

They wrote everything down:

```
   🌅 Morning: sun rises, wake up, breakfast
   ☀️ Afternoon: sun high, lunch, play
   🌙 Night: moon out, dinner, sleep
```

> "Now set the clock!"

Click, click, click — the hands moved to the right places. Ding-dong!

The sun went back to morning. The moon went back to night.

> "You fixed time!" cheered the clock keeper. "How did you know?"

> "Easy," said Alex. "Morning, afternoon, night — they always come in order. Today, tomorrow, and the next day too. Time is the most reliable friend — it never stops, never changes its order."

The clock tower chimed twelve times. It was noon — **afternoon** was beginning. And everything was right again.
