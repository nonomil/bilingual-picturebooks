## Page 1: The Big Question Game 🎲

After finding Ginger, the friends play a new game.

> "Let's play the Question Game! Ask me anything — but you must use a WH word!"

```
   🎲 WH Question Game
   
   Ask using: Who? What? When? Where? Why? How?
```

> "**Who** found the ladder?" asks Steve.
> "Tom did!" everyone answers.

```
   W-H-O → who 👤
   
   who = 谁 — asking about a person
```

> "**How** did Ginger get up the tree?" asks Alex.
> "She climbed it!" says Steve.

```
   H-O-W → how 🔧
   
   how = 怎么 — asking about the way
```

> "**Why** did Ginger run away?" asks Mia.

```
   W-H-Y → why 🤔
   
   why = 为什么 — asking about the reason
```

> "Maybe she saw a bird?" guesses Steve.

The questions keep coming:

```
   Who helped? → Tom! 👦
   What was lost? → Ginger the cat! 🐱
   When did we find her? → In the afternoon! ☀️
   Where was she? → In the big tree! 🌳
   Why was she there? → Chasing a bird? 🐦
   How did we get her down? → With a ladder! 🪜
```

## Page 2: The Detective Game 🕵️

Alex has a new idea. "Let's be detectives! We solve mysteries with questions!"

```
   🕵️ Detective Game
   
   Mystery: Who took the cookie from the cookie jar?
   
   Detective questions:
   Who was in the kitchen? 👤
   What is missing? ❓
   When did it happen? ⏰
   Where was the cookie jar? 📍
   Why would someone take it? 🤔
   How did they reach it? 🔧
```

They ask everyone in the village questions. Step by step, they solve the mystery:

> Cookie was taken at night. Only one person was awake. It was... THE CAT!

Ginger meows innocently. Everyone laughs.

> "Questions are the key to solving any mystery," says Alex.

### ⚠️ Common Mistakes

| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Right |
|----------|---------|
| "Where you live?" | **"Where do you live?"** — WH 问句需要助动词 do/does |
| "What you doing?" | **"What are you doing?"** — 现在进行时 = am/is/are + -ing |
| "Who is she? She is my friend." vs "Who is he?" | **不要混淆 he/she！** 中文"他/她"同音，英语不一样 |
| "How many years do you have?" | **"How old are you?"** — 年龄用 How old，不是 How many years |
### 🧠 Think About It
1. **Story**: In "The Lost Cat", why does Alex feel sad? What clues in the story tell you about her feelings?
2. **What if**: What if the treasure map in the last story was written in a language nobody could read? How would Steve and Alex solve the problem?
