## Page 1: The Magic Mirror 🪞

Back from the farm, Steve and Alex return to the village.

In the school, the teacher has a surprise.

> "Today, we will learn about... YOU!"

He pulls a cloth off a big object. It is a mirror — but not any mirror.

> "This is the Magic Mirror! Look into it, and you will see..."

Steve peers into the mirror. His reflection smiles back.

> "What do you see?" asks the teacher.

> "I see... me!"

> "Yes! And what parts do you have? Let's name them all."

```
   🧍 My body has:
   
   head    eyes    nose    mouth
   ears    hands   feet
```

**Sight word: am**
> "I **am** Steve. This is my body."

**am** = 是 — I am (only with "I")

```
   I am big.     I am little.
   I am happy.   I am a student.
```

## Page 8: Story Time — The Mixed-Up Monster 👾

That night, Steve has a funny dream.

A silly monster appears. But his body parts are all mixed up!

> "Help me!" cries the monster. "My ears are where my hands should be!"

```
   👂 Ears on his hands
   👀 Eyes on his feet
   👃 Nose on top of his head
   👄 Mouth where his ear should be!
```

> "Oh no! Let me help you!" says Dream Steve.

He puts the monster back together:

```
   👀 Eyes → on the HEAD
   👂 Ears → on the SIDES
   👃 Nose → in the MIDDLE
   👄 Mouth → below the nose
   🤲 Hands → on the arms
   🦶 Feet → at the bottom
```

> "Ahhh! Much better!" The monster looks in the mirror. "Now I can see with my eyes, hear with my ears, smell with my nose, and eat with my mouth!"

The monster dances happily. "Thank you, Steve!"

> "Remember," says Steve. "Every body part has a special job."

The monster nods. "And all of them together make... ME!"

Steve wakes up smiling.

### 2. What Do They Do?

| Body Part | What It Does |
|-----------|-------------|
| eyes | hear |
| ears | walk |
| nose | see |
| mouth | smell |
| hands | eat and talk |
| feet | hold and write |

Match correctly: eyes → see, ears → hear, etc.

### ⚠️ Common Mistakes

| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Right |
|----------|---------|
| "Touch you nose" | **"Touch your nose"** — your (你的) ≠ you (你) |
| "I have 2 foot" | **"I have 2 feet"** — foot → feet is irregular (不规则变化) |
| "My hands is big" | **"My hands are big"** — plural = are! (hands → are) |
| "I has two eyes" | **"I have two eyes"** — I/you/we/they → have |

### 🧠 Think About It
1. **Biology**: Why do we have two eyes but only one nose? What would be different if we had two noses?
2. **What if**: What if the Mixed-Up Monster mixed up YOUR body parts? Which mix-up would be the funniest?
