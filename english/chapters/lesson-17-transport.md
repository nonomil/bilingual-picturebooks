# Lesson 17: Let's Go! 🚗

## 📋 Learning Goals
- Transport words: **car, bus, train, boat, plane, bike**
- Sight words: **he, she, they, this, those**
- Sentence: "I go by ___." / "He/She goes by ___."
- 🔤 Sound Block: **tr and dr blends** (train, tree, truck / drive, drink, drop)

**Total words so far: 154** (L1-L16: 149, L17: +5 new)

---

## 🔤 Sound Block: tr and dr blends!

```
   🎵 NOTE BLOCK ACTIVATED!
   
   tr = /tr/  (like "choo-choo" start!)
   tr-ain → train! 🚂
   tr-ee → tree! 🌳
   tr-uck → truck! 🚛
   
   dr = /dr/  (like "vroom" start!)
   dr-ive → drive! 🚗
   dr-ink → drink! 🥤
   dr-op → drop! 💧
   
   Tap your tongue: tr tr tr!  dr dr dr!
```

## Page 1: How Do We Go? 🗺️

Steve and Alex want to go on a trip.

> "But how do we get there? There are so many ways to go!"

They look at a big board showing all the vehicles.

```
   🚗 Ways to Go:
   
   🚗 car       🚌 bus       🚂 train
   🚤 boat      ✈️ plane     🚲 bike
```

**Sight word: he**
> "**He** likes the car!"

**he** = 他 — a boy or man

```
   He is Steve. 👦
   He runs fast. 🏃
   He has a bike. 🚲
```

**Sight word: she**
> "**She** likes the bus!"

**she** = 她 — a girl or woman

```
   She is Alex. 👧
   She can swim. 🏊
   She takes the train. 🚂
```

## Page 2: Car and Bus 🚗🚌

> "Let's start with the **car**!" says Steve.

```
   C-A-R → car 🚗
```

> "A car has four wheels. It goes on roads. Many families have a car."

```
   🚗 Car:
   - four wheels
   - goes on roads
   - small, for a family
   
   Dad drives the car.
   "Vroom vroom!"
```

Steve pretends to drive. "Beep beep! I'm driving!"

> "But for going to school, I take the **bus**!"

```
   B-U-S → bus 🚌
```

> "A bus is a big vehicle. Many people can ride together."

```
   🚌 Bus:
   - big vehicle
   - many people
   - yellow school bus!
   
   "The bus stops here."
```

**Sight word: this**
> "**This** bus goes to the park!"

```
   This car is red.
   This is my bike.
   I like this!
```

## Page 3: Train 🚂

> "The best way to go far is by **train**!"

```
   T-R-A-I-N → train 🚂
```

> "A train runs on tracks. It goes CHOO-CHOO! It is very fast and can carry many people."

```
   🚂 Train:
   - runs on tracks 🛤️
   - long, many cars
   - very fast!
   - choo-choo! 🚂
```

Steve and Alex go to the train station. A big steam engine hisses at the platform.

> "All aboard!" calls the conductor.

They find seats by the window. The train whistle blows. WHOO-WHOO!

The countryside flies by — farms, forests, rivers. "Look! Cows!" "Look! A river!"

**🔤 Sound Block practice:**
```
   tr says /tr/!
   tr-ain → train! 🚂
   The TRAIN goes on TRACKS!
   
   tr-ee → tree! 🌳
   I see TREES from the TRAIN window!
```

**Sight word: they**
> "**They** are going to the city too!"

**they** = 他们 — more than one person

```
   They are friends.
   They like the train.
   Where are they going?
```

## Page 4: Boat 🚤

The train arrives at a big lake. There is a ferry dock.

> "Now we take a **boat**!"

```
   B-O-A-T → boat 🚤
```

> "A boat goes on water. It floats and can take you across lakes, rivers, and seas."

```
   🚤 Boat:
   - goes on water
   - floats 🌊
   - for lakes, rivers, sea
   
   "Row, row, row your boat..."
```

They hop on a small wooden boat. Steve rows the oars. Splish, splash!

The water is clear. Little fish swim under the boat.

> "This boat is slow — but it's so peaceful."

**Sight word: those**
> "Look at **those** fish under the water!"

**those** = 那些 — things over there (more than one)

```
   This fish (近) → These fish (近，多)
   That fish (远) → Those fish (远，多)
   
   Those birds are flying.
   I like those flowers.
```

## Page 5: Plane ✈️

At the other side of the lake, they see something in the sky.

> "Look up! A **plane**!"

```
   P-L-A-N-E → plane ✈️
```

> "A plane flies in the sky. It is the fastest way to go very far!"

```
   ✈️ Plane:
   - flies in the sky
   - very fast!
   - for very far places
   
   The plane is above the clouds! ☁️
```

Steve spreads his arms like wings. "Zoom! I'm a plane!"

Alex laughs. "You're not a plane — you're Steve!"

They watch the plane draw a white line across the blue sky.

> "Someday I want to fly in a plane," says Alex. "All the way around the world!"

**🔤 Sound Block practice:**
```
   dr says /dr/!
   dr-ive → drive! 🚗
   You DRIVE a car, but you fly a plane!
   
   dr-eam → dream! 💭
   I DREAM of flying!
```

Back in the village, Steve and Alex hop on their favorite ride.

> "And my favorite — my **bike**!"

   B-I-K-E → bike 🚲

## Page 7: Story Time — The Great Transport Race 🏁

The village decided to have a race! But not a running race — a transport race.

> "Each team picks a vehicle. First one to the Big Mountain wins!"

Team Steve picked a bike. Team Alex picked a boat (the race goes through a river).

```
   🏁 Transport Race:
   
   🚲 Steve — bike (on the road)
   🚤 Alex — boat (on the river)
   🚗 Tom — car (on the road)
   🐴 Lily — horse (cross-country!)
```

BANG! The race starts!

Steve pedals hard on his bike. The road winds through the forest.
Alex rows fast on the river. The current helps push her boat.

At the crossroads, they meet up and wave. "Keep going!"

Steve pedals up a hill — huff, puff! — then zooms down fast!
Alex navigates around rocks in the river.

In the end, they both reach the Big Mountain at the same time!

> "We both win!" says Steve, panting.

> "Different vehicles, same fun!" says Alex.

The lesson: Every way of moving has its own adventure. Car, bus, train, boat, plane, bike — each one takes you somewhere new.

## 📝 Practice

### 1. Match the Transport

| Vehicle | Where | How |
|---------|-------|-----|
| 🚗 car | on tracks | pedal |
| 🚌 bus | in sky | many people |
| 🚂 train | on water | drive |
| 🚤 boat | on road | many people |
| ✈️ plane | on road | row or motor |
| 🚲 bike | on road | fly |

Draw lines to fix the table!

### 2. Fill In

```
   I _______ a car. (drive / fly)
   I _______ a plane. (fly / ride)
   I _______ a bike. (ride / drive)
   I _______ a boat. (row / fly)
```
