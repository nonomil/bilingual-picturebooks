# Lesson 11 Extension: Seasons of the Year 🍂🌸☀️❄️

## 📋 Learning Goals
- Review weather: sun, rain, cloud, wind, snow, hot, cold
- Review sight words: do, did, soon, that, under
- New words: **spring, summer, fall, winter, season**
- Sentence: "In ___, it is ___."
- 🔤 Sound Block review: ee /iː/ + ea says /iː/ too!

---

## 🔤 Sound Block Review: ee and ea say /iː/!

```
   🎵 NOTE BLOCK GLOWING!

   Two ways to write /iː/!

   ee says /iː/:  see 👀  tree 🌳  bee 🐝
   
   ea also says /iː/: (new!)
   s-ea → sea! 🌊
   l-ea-f → leaf! 🍃
   r-ea-d → read! 📖
   t-ea → tea! 🍵
   b-ea-ch → beach! 🏖️

   ee and ea — same sound, different letters!
   /iː/ /iː/ /iː/!
```

## Page 1: Four Seasons 🌈

After the weather report, the teacher gathers the class.

> "You know the weather. But did you know weather changes with the **seasons**?"

```
   S-E-A-S-O-N → season 🗓️
   
   There are FOUR seasons in a year:
   🌸 spring    ☀️ summer
   🍂 fall      ❄️ winter
```

> "Each season has its own weather. Let's explore them!"

```
   🗓️ Four Seasons:
   
   🌸 SPRING — flowers bloom, rain falls
   ☀️ SUMMER — sun is hot, days are long
   🍂 FALL — leaves change color, wind blows
   ❄️ WINTER — snow falls, it is cold
```

## Page 2: Spring 🌸

> "**Spring** is the first season. Everything wakes up!"

```
   S-P-R-I-N-G → spring 🌸
```

> "In spring, the snow melts. Flowers bloom. Baby animals are born."

```
   🌸 Spring:
   ☀️ warm sun
   🌧️ lots of rain
   🌱 new plants grow
   🐦 birds come back
   🌸 flowers bloom
```

The teacher takes them outside. "Look! Tiny green leaves on the trees!"

Alex kneels by a flower bed. "And flowers! Red, yellow, purple..."

A bird sings in the tree. A butterfly floats by.

> "Spring is when the world turns green again!"

**🔤 Sound Block:**
```
   ea says /iː/!
   l-ea-f → leaf! 🍃
   New leaves in spring!
```

## Page 3: Summer ☀️

> "After spring comes **summer**. The hottest season!"

```
   S-U-M-M-E-R → summer ☀️
```

> "In summer, the sun is very strong. Days are LONG. We can play outside all day!"

```
   ☀️ Summer:
   ☀️ hot sun
   🌡️ high temperature
   🏖️ go to the beach
   🍉 eat cool fruit
   🏊 swim in water
```

> "I love summer!" says Steve. "We can swim and eat ice cream!"

The teacher nods. "But remember — when it is hot, drink lots of water!"

They sit under a big tree, drinking water and fanning themselves.

> "Summer is fun, but it is SO hot!"

## Page 4: Fall 🍂

> "Then comes **fall**. The most colorful season!"

```
   F-A-L-L → fall 🍂
   
   (In some places, people say "autumn")
```

> "In fall, the weather gets cool. The leaves change color — red, orange, yellow, brown!"

```
   🍂 Fall:
   💨 cool wind
   🍂 leaves fall from trees
   🍎 apples ready to pick
   🌡️ not too hot, not too cold
```

Leaves crunch under their feet. Steve picks up a red maple leaf.

> "Look at this one! It is like fire!"

Alex gathers leaves of different colors. "Red, orange, yellow, brown — a rainbow of leaves!"

The wind blows and leaves dance in the air.

```
   🍂 In fall, trees go to sleep.
   They drop their leaves
   And wait for spring. 🌸
```

## Page 5: Winter ❄️

> "Last comes **winter**. The coldest season!"

```
   W-I-N-T-E-R → winter ❄️
```

> "In winter, it is very cold. Snow falls. The trees have no leaves."

```
   ❄️ Winter:
   🥶 very cold
   ❄️ snow falls
   ⛄ make snowmen
   🧥 wear warm coats
   🛷 go sledding
```

Steve shivers. "But winter is fun! Snowball fights!"

Alex nods. "And hot soup by the fire!"

> "After winter..." the teacher pauses. "Spring comes again! The seasons go around and around — like a circle."

```
   🔄 Seasons Circle:
   
   🌸 Spring → ☀️ Summer → 🍂 Fall → ❄️ Winter → 🌸
   
   Round and round,
   Year after year,
   Each season brings
   Its own good cheer!
```

## Page 6: My Favorite Season 🎨

The teacher hands out paper and crayons.

> "Draw your favorite season! Write why you love it."

Steve draws summer — bright sun, blue water, himself swimming.

```
   ☀️ My favorite season is SUMMER.
   I can swim and play outside all day.
   The sun is hot and the sky is blue.
   Summer is FUN!
```

Alex draws winter — white snow, a snowman, herself sledding.

```
   ❄️ My favorite season is WINTER.
   I love snow! I make snowmen.
   I drink hot soup by the fire.
   Winter is cozy!
```

They hang their drawings on the wall. Four seasons, four pictures, four stories.

## 📝 Practice

### 1. Which Season?

```
   Flowers bloom, birds come back. → ___
   Hot sun, long days, swimming. → ___
   Leaves change color, cool wind. → ___
   Snow falls, very cold. → ___
```

### 2. Fill In

```
   In SPRING, it is ___ and rainy.     (cold / warm)
   In SUMMER, it is ___ and sunny.     (hot / cold)
   In FALL, it is ___ and windy.       (hot / cool)
   In WINTER, it is ___ and snowy.     (cold / hot)
```
