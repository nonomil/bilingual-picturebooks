# Lesson 12: Clothes 👕

## 📋 Learning Goals
- Clothes words: **shirt, pants, shoes, hat, coat, dress**
- Sight words: **at, with, will, went, was**
- Sentence: "I wear ___." / "This is my ___."
- 🔤 Sound Block: **oo says /ʊ/** (book, look, good, foot, cook, wood)

**Total words so far: 108** (L1-L11: 104, L12: +4 new)

## Page 1: What to Wear? 👗

Steve wakes up and opens his closet. "Hmm... what should I wear today?"

```
   wear = 穿、戴
   clothes = 衣服
```

Alex knocks. "Steve! It's cold outside. Wear warm clothes!"

They look at all the different clothes:

```
   👕 CLOTHES 👕
   
   👔 shirt     👖 pants     👟 shoes
   🧢 hat       🧥 coat      👗 dress
```

**Sight word: at**
> "Look **at** all these clothes!"

**at** = 在、看 — pointing to something

```
   Look at the shirt. 👔
   Look at me! 👀
   I am at school. 🏫
```

## Page 2: Shirt and Pants 👔👖

Steve pulls out a blue top.

> "This is my **shirt**."

```
   S-H-I-R-T → shirt 👔
```

> "A shirt covers the top part of your body. It has sleeves for your arms."

```
   👔 Shirt:
   - top part of clothes
   - has sleeves
   - many colors!
```

He puts on his favorite blue shirt. "Perfect!"

Then he grabs his bottoms.

> "And these are my **pants**."

```
   P-A-N-T-S → pants 👖
```

> "Pants cover your legs. Everyone wears pants!"

```
   👖 Pants:
   - cover your legs
   - two leg holes
   - blue, black, brown...
```

**Sight word: with**
> "I wear my blue shirt **with** brown pants."

**with** = 和…一起 — together

```
   Shirt with pants. 👔👖
   Play with friends. 👫
   Milk with cookies. 🥛🍪
```

## Page 3: Shoes 👟

Steve sits down to put on his footwear.

> "Time for my **shoes**!"

```
   S-H-O-E-S → shoes 👟
   
   One shoe 👟  Two shoes 👟👟
```

> "Shoes protect your feet. You wear shoes outside."

```
   👟 Shoes:
   - protect your feet
   - wear them outside
   - tie the laces! 🎀
```

Steve ties his shoelaces carefully. Loop, swoop, and pull!

> "Left shoe, right shoe — both on!"

**Sight word: will**
> "I **will** wear my shoes all day."

**will** = 将会 — going to, in the future

```
   I will go outside. 🚶
   I will play with you. ⚽
   It will be fun! 🎉
```

Steve stomps his feet. "Ready!"

## Page 4: Hat and Coat 🧢🧥

Alex comes in wearing warm clothes.

> "Look at my **hat**!"

```
   H-A-T → hat 🧢
```

> "A hat goes on your head. It keeps you warm — or cool!"

```
   🧢 Hat:
   - goes on your head
   - keeps sun out of eyes ☀️
   - keeps head warm in winter ❄️
```

Then she shows her outer layer.

> "And this is my **coat**!"

```
   C-O-A-T → coat 🧥
```

> "A coat keeps you warm when it is cold outside. It goes over your shirt."

```
   🧥 Coat:
   - goes over your shirt
   - keeps you warm
   - for cold weather 🥶
```

**Sight word: went**
> "Yesterday I **went** outside without a coat. I was so cold!"

**went** = 去了（go 的过去式）

```
   I went to school. 🏫
   She went to the park. 🌳
   We went home. 🏠
```

## Page 5: Dress 👗

At school, a girl student shows her outfit.

> "I am wearing a **dress** today!"

```
   D-R-E-S-S → dress 👗
```

> "A dress is one piece of clothing. It covers the top and bottom together."

The dress is pink with little white flowers. The girl twirls — the dress spins around!

```
   👗 Dress:
   - one piece (top + bottom together)
   - many girls wear dresses
   - can be long or short
   - for parties and special days 🎉
```

**Sight word: was**
> "The dress **was** made by my grandma!"

**was** = 是/在（is/am 的过去式）

```
   It was cold yesterday. 🥶
   I was at school. 🏫
   The dress was pretty. 👗
```

Alex admires the dress. "It is so beautiful!"

```
   🎯 Sight words review:
   AT — look AT the dress!
   WITH — a dress WITH flowers
   WILL — I WILL wear a dress too
   WENT — I WENT to buy it
   WAS — it WAS made with love
```

## Page 7: Story Time — The Mixed-Up Closet 👔

That night, Steve's closet makes a strange sound.

> "Psst! Psst!"

Steve opens the door. The clothes are ALIVE!

```
   👔 Shirt: "I want to be worn TODAY!"
   👖 Pants: "No, me! ME!"
   🧢 Hat: "I should go on the feet!"
   👟 Shoes: "I should go on the head!"
```

The clothes are all mixed up! Hat on feet. Shoes on head. Coat inside out!

> "Stop! Stop!" says Steve. "Clothes have special places!"

He sorts them out:

```
   🧢 Hat → on the HEAD ✓
   👔 Shirt → on the TOP ✓
   🧥 Coat → over the shirt ✓
   👖 Pants → on the LEGS ✓
   👟 Shoes → on the FEET ✓
```

The clothes sigh happily. "Ahhh, that feels right!"

> "Thank you, Steve," says the shirt. "We just wanted to be useful."

> "You are," Steve says. "Every piece of clothing has a job. You keep me warm, dry, and comfortable."

The clothes glow with pride. "We WILL do our job well!"

When Steve wakes up in the morning, his clothes are neatly folded on the chair. All in the right order: hat, shirt, coat, pants, shoes.

> "Was it a dream?" Steve wonders with a smile.
