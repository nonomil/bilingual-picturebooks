### ⚠️ Common Mistakes

| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Right |
|----------|---------|
| "I wish you happy birthday" | **"I wish you a happy birthday"** — wish someone a + 名词 |
| "Let's celebrate" vs "Let's to celebrate" | **"Let's celebrate"** — Let's + 动词原形 |
| "The balloon red" | **"The red balloon"** — 形容词在名词前面！ |
| "I give you a gift" vs "I gift you" | **"I give you a gift"** — gift 是名词，不是动词（give = 动词） |
### 🧠 Think About It
1. **Story**: In "The Lost Cat", why does Alex feel sad? What clues in the story tell you about her feelings?

## Page 2: Party Time! 🎊

Friday arrives. The playground is transformed!

```
   🎊 Party Day
   
   🎈 Balloons everywhere
   🎵 Music playing
   🎂 Big cake on the table
   📸 Photo corner
   🎨 Face painting
```

Everyone comes — students, teachers, even the farmer from the storm!

> "Let's **celebrate**!" shouts the teacher.

They play games. They **sing** songs. They eat **cake**. They dance.

At the end, everyone gathers at the wish tree.

> "My **wish** is for everyone to be **happy**," says Steve.

> "My **wish** is for more parties like this," says Alex.

The teacher smiles. "The best way to **celebrate** is together."
