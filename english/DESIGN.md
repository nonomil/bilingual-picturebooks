# English Picturebook Design (Kindergarten Series)

> Following the same style as Math & Chinese series: Steve & Alex Minecraft adventures, story-based learning, 12 lessons + 12 extensions
> 2026-05-13 | Updated with Dolch Sight Word benchmarks

## Reference: Dolch Sight Words

## Curriculum (12 base + 12 extension)

### Goal: ~45 sight words (Pre-Primer equivalent)

| Lesson | Topic | Core Content | New Words (Dolch alignment) |
|--------|-------|-------------|----------------------------|
| 1 | Hello! | Greetings, self-intro | hello, goodbye, name, friend, ***you*** |
| 2 | ABC 1 | Letters A-M | apple, ball, cat, dog, ***I, is, in, it*** |
| 3 | ABC 2 | Letters N-Z | ***my, not, one, the, two, up, we*** |
| 4 | Colors | red blue yellow green | ***a, big, blue, red, yellow, look, see*** |
| 5 | Numbers | one to six | ***and, can, come, down, find, for, three*** |
| 6 | Animals | cat dog bird fish rabbit | ***help, here, little, play, run, said*** |
| 7 | My Body | head eyes nose mouth ears hands feet | ***all, am, are, have, he, she, make*** |
| 8 | My Family | mom dad sister brother baby | ***at, like, good, want, went, will, with*** |
| 9 | Food & Drinks | apple banana milk bread egg cake water | ***eat, get, no, now, on, out, was*** |
| 10 | Toys & School | ball doll car book pencil bag | ***away, funny, jump, me, must, new, this*** |
| 11 | Weather | sun rain cloud wind snow | ***do, did, soon, that, there, too, under*** |
| 12 | Review | All words + story | ***where, white, yes, please, pretty*** |

### Word alignment notes

Each lesson teaches **3-5 Dolch sight words** (bolded above) embedded in a story context, plus **4-6 content/theme words**. By Lesson 12, all 40 Pre-Primer words are covered, plus ~20 theme words = ~60 words total.

Every Dolch word appears in **at least 2 lessons** (spaced repetition).

## Image Prompts

Base style (same as Math):
```
Minecraft pixel art style, white background,
Steve (blue shirt, dark pants, brown hair) and Alex (orange shirt, jeans)
in a blocky world, children educational illustration,
clear simple shapes, bright colors
```

Scene additions:
- **Alphabet**: big letter with matching object, word label
- **Colors**: objects in single color on white
- **Numbers**: counted objects clearly visible
- **Dialogues**: speech bubbles with text, simple scene
