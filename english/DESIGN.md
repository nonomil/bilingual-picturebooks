# English Picturebook Design (Kindergarten Series)

> Following the same style as Math & Chinese series: Steve & Alex Minecraft adventures, story-based learning, 12 lessons + 12 extensions
> See `../chinese-language/DESIGN.md` for full design specifications

## Quick Reference

### Curriculum Overview

| Lesson | Topic | Core Content | New Words |
|--------|-------|-------------|-----------|
| 1 | Hello! | Greetings, self-introduction | hello, goodbye, name, friend |
| 2 | ABC Adventure | Alphabet A-M | apple, ball, cat, dog, egg, fish, girl, hat, igloo, juice, kite, lion, moon |
| 3 | ABC Adventure 2 | Alphabet N-Z | nest, orange, pig, queen, rabbit, sun, tree, umbrella, van, water, fox, yellow, zebra |
| 4 | Colorful World | Colors | red, blue, yellow, green, black, white, pink, purple |
| 5 | Number Fun | Numbers 1-10 | one, two, three, four, five, six, seven, eight, nine, ten |
| 6 | Animal Friends | Animals | cat, dog, bird, fish, rabbit, cow, pig, duck, frog |
| 7 | My Body | Body parts | head, eyes, nose, mouth, ears, hands, feet, arms |
| 8 | My Family | Family | mom, dad, sister, brother, baby, grandma, grandpa |
| 9 | Yummy Food | Food & drinks | apple, banana, milk, bread, egg, cake, water, juice, cookie |
| 10 | My Toys | Toys & school | ball, doll, car, book, pencil, bag, crayon, blocks |
| 11 | Weather Fun | Weather & nature | sunny, rainy, cloudy, windy, snowy, hot, cold, rainbow |
| 12 | Party Time! | Full review | All words |

### Image Prompts

Base style (same as Math):
```
Minecraft pixel art style, white background,
Steve (blue shirt, dark pants, brown hair) and Alex (orange shirt, jeans)
in a blocky world, children educational illustration,
clear simple shapes, bright colors
```

Scene additions:
- **Alphabet**: big letter with matching object, word label
- **Colors**: objects in single color on white
- **Numbers**: counted objects clearly visible
- **Dialogues**: speech bubbles with text, simple scene
